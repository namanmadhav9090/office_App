UI Flow Overview
Authentication Screens

Login Screen: Allows users to log in.
Signup Screen: Allows users to register.
Dashboard Screens (For Managers)

Department Dashboard: Displays departments with pagination.
Department Details: Shows detailed info of a selected department.
Create/Update Department: Form to create or update a department.
Employee Management Screens

Assign Employees: Assigns employees to a department.
